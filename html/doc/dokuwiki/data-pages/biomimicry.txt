====== Biomimicry ======

Three types.

1. Mimic the structure and locomotion of animals.  Robots.  Snake, bird, butterfly.  Biped, quadraped, six-legged insects, eight-legged spiders.

2. Mimic the brain, as in the neural net.

3. Mimic evolution: natural selection: breeding, random mutations, survival of the fittest.  As in genetic algorithms.
